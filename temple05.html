📜 History

An ancient Shiva temple, believed to be over 1000 years old Associated with the Chola dynasty, known for building temples in this region The temple has been a center of continuous worship for centuries

🔱 Mythology

Lord Shiva is worshipped here as Kuzhagar (Amirthakadeswarar) The place is believed to be sacred where devas worshipped Shiva Strong spiritual connection due to its sea-facing location

🏛️ Architecture

  • Simple yet ancient granite structure
  • Traditional garbhagriha (sanctum)
  • Calm and less crowded atmosphere

🌊 Historical Importance

Reflects the rich temple heritage of the Chola period Part of the cluster of sacred temples in Thirukkuvalai region Preserves local Tamil religious traditions.

🙏 Festivals

  • Mahashivaratri (major celebration)
  • Pradosham special poojas
  • Local festivals observed with devotion
